Welcome

Shreepad hospital is one of the largest, advanced and best ENT service providing hospital in Nasik India. We are one of leading hospital as far as ENT in Nasik is concerned. We aim to provide the best ENT medical support and care with full commitment to ensure that every patient receives required treatment in India in general and Nasik in particular with complete compassion. This is our primary goal as well as commitment.

The hospital is led by the founder Dr. Mahesh Nikam. He is not just the founder, but also one of the senior most and renowned ENT surgeons in Nasik.We deal with all the major diseases related with the ear, nose, and throat by the use of sophisticated and modern technology. Apart from common surgery like Myringoplasty, Ossssiculoplasty, Balloon Sinuplasty, Endoscopic DCR, Adeniodectonomy, Tonsillectomy, we also handle and deal with many other surgeries in the hospital.

Our hospital is well facilitated with different departments for OPD, Casualty, ICU, Surgery. Anyone with unique problem is taken in separate care in an in-patient room which is wide, clean and hygienic. Almost all of the advanced technologies needed for ENT treatment are available in the hospital. We aim to serve people with the best quality service at the best possible price.
